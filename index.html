My Favorite characters in Snowpiercer

Character Characteristics Why I like them
Curtis
  • Passionate
  • Strong
 I love Curtis for how he became a better man after Gilliam taught him how
to be a better man, and after he turned a new leaf he would be the one to
lead a revolt on the Snowpiercer
Gilliam
  • Generous
  • Teacher/Guiding light
 I love Gilliam since he acted like a Guiding light for the people in the caboose
of the train since he would show them values like generosity and
kindness by cutting his arm to feed others.
